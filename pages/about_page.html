About Cewen Chi

Quantitative Analyst & Computational Finance Specialist

Professional Philosophy

My journey in quantitative finance began with a deep fascination for the mathematical elegance underlying financial markets. With a Master of Science in Computational Finance (MSCF) foundation, I've dedicated my career to bridging the gap between theoretical financial models and practical implementation in high-stakes trading environments.

I believe that successful quantitative analysis requires not just mathematical rigor, but also intuitive understanding of market dynamics and human behavior. This philosophy drives my approach to developing robust, interpretable models that perform consistently across different market regimes while maintaining transparency for risk management teams.

My passion lies in algorithmic trading strategies that leverage advanced statistical methods, machine learning techniques, and stochastic calculus to identify and exploit market inefficiencies. I'm particularly drawn to problems involving high-dimensional data, non-linear relationships, and time-series forecasting in financial contexts.

MSCF Program Background

The Master of Science in Computational Finance program provided me with a comprehensive foundation in quantitative methods, combining rigorous mathematical training with practical programming skills essential for modern financial analysis. The curriculum emphasized both theoretical understanding and hands-on application of complex financial models.
